\chapter{Q-criterion contour matrices at \texttt{ConeEnd}}
\label{app:qcrit-contours}
\fancyhead{}

This appendix shows the full \(Q\)-criterion contour matrices at \texttt{ConeEnd} for the constant-chord (CC) and constant-solidity (CS) guide-vane families. The figures support the discussion in Section~\ref{sec:q-criterion-analysis}. The contour fields are used for qualitative comparison of coherent vortex-core regions between the guide-vane configurations.
