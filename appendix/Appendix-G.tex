\chapter{Appendix G: Dean-vortex diagnostics}
\label{app:dean-panels}
\fancyhead{}

This appendix contains the additional secondary-flow diagnostics used to support
Section~\ref{sec:bend-hl-collapse}. The figures are interpreted qualitatively.
The bend stations are not equally spaced in arclength, and the post-bend
coverage is limited to two pipe diameters.
