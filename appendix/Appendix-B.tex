\chapter{Three-dimensional guide-vane geometries}
\label{app:gv_3d_geometries}
\fancyhead{}

This appendix collects the three-dimensional renderings of all 18 active guide-vane configurations of the screening matrix (Table~\ref{tab:design_matrix}). The renderings are produced by the parametric MATLAB tool \texttt{vanedesign\_loft\_export}~\cite{Leonsen2025} after the spanwise inputs $\{r,\,C_m(r),\,C_u(r)\}$ from the RDT wake have been used to set the metal angles, the camber and the wrap. All renderings use the same axial-view orientation; the rotor is to the left and the duct flow proceeds to the right. The plotted vane height matches the design span $r \in [0.12,\,0.25]$~m. The matched pair \texttt{CC\_400\_8}/\texttt{CS\_400\_8} discussed in Section~\ref{sec:theory_const_chord_const_sol} is included here for completeness, in addition to the 16 remaining cases.

The figures are grouped by design family. The constant-chord (CC) family is presented first, followed by the constant-solidity (CS) family. Within each family, the cases are ordered by ascending shroud chord and then by ascending vane count.
