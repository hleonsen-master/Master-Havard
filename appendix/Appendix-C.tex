\chapter{Bend secondary-flow supplementary figures}
\label{app:dean-panels}
\fancyhead{}
This appendix supplements Sections~\ref{sec:dean-vortex} and~\ref{sec:bend-hl-collapse}
with additional secondary-flow contours at the bend inlet and downstream of the bend exit.
The figures are qualitative diagnostics. The bend stations are not equally spaced
in arclength, and the post-bend coverage is limited to two pipe diameters.
The \texttt{BendEnd} plane and the streamwise development are shown in the main text,
Figures~\ref{fig:dean-montage} and~\ref{fig:dean-streamwise}. The colour scale and
annotations follow Figure~\ref{fig:dean-montage}.
